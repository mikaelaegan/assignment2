One4all competition

In college, we put ourselves forward to be in a competition for the One4all voucher. Here, I designed a card that represented a newborn baby. This was a lot of fun. I planned out my design in a sketchbook and then from progressed on to using Adobe illustrator to digitially produce my design. The end result turned out quite nice. I stuck to the same colour palette to make it more visually appealing
